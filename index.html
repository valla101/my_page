NBA Awards Predictor

Ever wondered how to apply machine learning? This is an example of how to combine sports and technology! The scope of the project was to use statistics from previous seasons’ winners, apply these statistics into algorithms to make the machine “learn”, and subsequently predict the current season’s award winners. In this project we were able to predict Most Valuable Player and Rookie of The Year. Our predictions were correct! Giannis Antetokounmpo won Most Valuable Player and Ja Morant won Rookie of The Year for the 2019-2020 NBA Season.
